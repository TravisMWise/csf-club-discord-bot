What is the Collegiate Space Foundation?:
  CSF is a NASA sponsored student-run organization created to provide students with the opportunity to develop their skills through the engineering of space-based technologies. We actively work with multiple advisors from UCF and NASA to assist in R&D and streamline members into research positions. 

When are meetings? 
  Our members meet bi-weekly on Wednesday at 6PM. When we have open-meetings, we will send an announcement in the general text channel. 
 
Where are the meetings?
  In a zoom session. We are working on coordinating monthly in-person meetings open to everyone.
